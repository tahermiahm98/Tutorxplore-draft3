Welcome To TutorXplore
TutorXplore is an online tutoring website designed to help students study science. We understand that learning can be challenging, so we've created a platform to help you keep up with your studies and reach your academic goals.
What We Offer
TutorXplore focuses on targeting students to become well knowledged in all three Sciences; Biology, Chemistry and Physics. Preparing them for their GCSEs by providing students with key exam knowledge to help them succeed.
We do this by incorporating highly successful strategies to help maximise effective learning to take place in students. Online videos which use high quality visual presentations alongside live commentary to help keep students engaged and make learning fun. Learning materials which have been carefully created to allow students to attempt multiple tasks on all topics, helping to deepen their knowledge and grow their confidence in Science.
